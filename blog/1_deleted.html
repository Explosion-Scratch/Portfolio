Should we be able to view deleted comments?

9/23/2020

Recently I've been working on a cool browser extension for scratch called Scratch Addons it basically gives people scratch superpowers, such as dark mode on the whole website, left side stage, searchable dropdowns and more. Another cool thing? Deleted comments.

Scratch Addons recently removed an addon which allowed users to view deleted comments and their poster. While this was super cool and all, is that the right thing to do? I mean they were deleted for a reason...

In my opinion, I don't think that deleted comments were meant to be seen, but it is really cool (And sort of sad!) that it's possible. I really think it's cool so I still use the addon, but I agree that it should not be released or shared.

In conclusion, even though it's super cool, viewing deleted comments is just, not right. I still haven't gotten over the amazingness of it though so I am still using it. Perhaps I'll remove it in the future! Thanks for reading!
